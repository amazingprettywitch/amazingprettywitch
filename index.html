Isn't Every Love Poem An Unfinished Love Poem

Praise the ear.
Praise the hair curling
around the ear.

Praise the music
we never turn on,
only make.

Praise the caps
of your shoulders, my lips
pressed against them

Praise the poem
I was trying to finish
when you showed up

at my door

he has a way of honouring my body
honestly and gently
i'm embraced by a genuine and intimate appreciation
for my essence and compassion
kissing him is like meditation
i can feel my presence

i like to kiss him slow
so we can feel the way our lips move together
i like to pause and pull away
to feel the magnet that quickly pulls my lips back to his
i like to watch his expressions as i explore his body
to make me want him more
i feel the magic in our slightest touch
the force of intention between our skin
stronger than simple contact
i crave the anticipation
and bathe in our satisfaction

when our bodies share space
we can make anything happen
when we make a wish we know it will come true
